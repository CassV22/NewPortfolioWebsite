Etch-A-Sketch

Etch-A-Sketch

A simple sketchpad or Etch-A-Sketch website made using HTML, CSS, and Javascript. To use, click on a grid size to choose the size of your grid or to reset your canvas. Then choose a color from the color pallet or click the black button for black or click the multi-color button to get a series of random colors. To start drawing, once a color is chosen click on a square and drag your mouse. Click again on your final square to stop drawing.

Rock Paper Scissors

Rock Paper Scissors

A classic game of rock paper scissors created using HTML/CSS and Javascript. Click on the rock, paper, or scissor button to choose your move, then wait for the computer to make it's move. The first one to 5 points wins the game.

JS Drumkit

JS Drumkit

Use your keyboard to rock out with this Javascript drum kit. Each key on the screen is connected to a part of a drum kit; press the key to hear the corresponding sound. Press the keys quickly to connect each sound as if you're playing a real drum kit.

Nucamp Portfolio Project

Nucamp Portfolio Project

This project is a work in progress; it is currently in the design stage. The project is inteded to be an educational website for students that allow them to explore and learn about STEM concepts in fun ways throughout their summer break. Users will be able to log into their account, play STEM games, find and purchase STEM-related books from educational reading lists, learn about different STEM professions, and much more.

Odin Recipes - The Chocolate Lover's Cookbook

The first project that I worked on while completing the Odin Project. A simple, static website that features several recipes that chocolate lovers can enjoy. To view the recipes, click on the chocolate lover image at the center of the website's homepage to get started.
